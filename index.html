Product

Everything your team
needs to move fast.

OurUFO fuses spatial computing with generative AI to create an immersive, intelligent collaboration paradigm.

🏠

UFO Room

WebXR-powered virtual studios featuring 3D whiteboards, spatial sticky notes, and real-time co-editing. Pair with AR glasses for seamless overlay on your physical environment.

🤖

OmniAI Assistant

Live transcription, 42-language translation, auto-generated action items, and predictive risk flagging based on your team's communication history.

⚡

FlowSync

Deep integration with Jira, Notion, Slack, GitHub, and 200+ tools. AI automatically syncs task status across platforms, eliminating information silos entirely.

👁

Presence Engine

Lightweight camera and microphone analysis intelligently detects focus states, schedules meetings at optimal times, and prevents low-value interruptions.

📊

Insight Dashboard

Deep analytics on collaboration patterns with visual efficiency reports and AI-driven improvement recommendations tailored to your team's workflows.

🔌

Open API

Full REST & WebSocket APIs with comprehensive docs, SDKs, and enterprise SSO. Build custom integrations and extend OurUFO to fit any workflow.
